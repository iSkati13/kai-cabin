Cancellation Policy

  1. Standard Cancellation Terms
    • Full Refund: Cancel 30 days or more before your check-in date to get a 100% refund of all payments.
    • Partial Refund: If you cancel between 14 and 29 days before check-in, you'll receive a 50% refund of your total booking.
    • Rebooking: You can rebook your stay up to two times, but this must be done at least 14 days before your original check-in date and is subject to availability.
    • Important: Change requests made 14 days or less before your booked date are not eligible for a refund or rebooking.
  2. Acts of Nature
    • In cases of unforeseen circumstances beyond your control (like natural disasters or government-imposed travel restrictions), our rebooking policy will apply.
  3. Early Checkout
    • No refunds will be provided for early checkout or no-shows.
  4. Security Deposit
    • The security deposit is fully refundable, provided no damages are incurred. Refunds are processed within a day after check-out.
  5. Cancellation Procedure
    • To cancel your reservation:
      Submit a written cancellation request to info@kaiscabin.com or send us a private message on our social media channels:
      facebook.com/kaiscabin
      IG @kais.cabin
      Whatsapp/Viber - 09339566221
    • Include your booking date/s and full name.
    • Await a confirmation email acknowledging the cancellation and detailing any applicable refunds.

How to Get Here

Private Vehicle (From Manila or North Luzon)
  1. Exit NLEX at Bocaue.
  2. From Manila: Turn right onto Gov. Fortunato Halili Ave.
    From North Luzon: Turn left onto Gov. Fortunato Halili Ave.
  3. Continue straight, pass BMMG Hospital, Mateo Hospital.
  4. Turn left onto Santa Maria Bypass Road (Landmarks: PTT, Shakey's, Contis, Peri Peri).
  5. Merge onto Norzagaray - Santa Maria Road (Landmark: Waltermart Sta. Maria).
  6. Go straight, pass Goldilocks, Philhealth, RePhil Gas station.
  7. Turn onto City Land Drive (Landmarks: AllHome, Jollibee, McDo).
  8. Pin Pulong Buhangin Barangay Hall for Waze/Google Maps.
  9. Straight along Cityland Drive, turn left on Lopez Jaena before Divine Mercy chapel to reach Kai's Cabin.
Commute
  1. Ride a bus at Trinoma P2P terminal bound to Sta. Maria (Precious Grace Transport).
  2. Drop at Sta. Clara or Caypombo, then ride a Jeep/Bus to Norzagaray.
  3. Ask to be dropped at City Land or Pulong Buhangin Barangay Hall.
  4. Ride a trike to Kai's Cabin.
